Project 01

PMEI - Partner Marketing Execution Insights

Shopper Journey & Experience Tracker

Role

Project Manager (Team Lead)

Users

Microsoft partner marketing, category, and analytics stakeholders

Ownership

End-to-end ownership from data crawl to front-end development, QC, and client-facing POC management

Business Impact

Annual revenue of $900K

PMEI provides a unified, data-driven view of OEM shopper journey, digital performance, and experience signals. Built on the C5i Compete platform and tailored for Microsoft, it helps users move from executive snapshot to deeper OEM, country, and SKU-level analysis.

The platform combines executive KPI readouts with structured drilldowns across shopper journey, digital performance, ratings and reviews, influencer tracking, and GenAI-based insights.

Executive Summary

Quick read on clicks to purchase, digital performance, ratings and reviews, and social media snapshot.

Digital Performance

Benchmark OEM and country-level website performance and evaluate how the consumer journey is performing.

Ratings & Reviews

Monitor review volume and sentiment mix to understand consumer perception at a glance.

SKU Analysis

SKU-level view of digital journey performance and product-level analysis across each shopper stage.

Influencer Tracking

Review influencer presence and themes such as AI readiness, productivity, reliability, and security.

GenAI Insights

AI-generated insights on OEM websites, highlighting strengths, weaknesses, and newsfeed updates.

Project 02

Global Retailers Real Time Price Tracking & Analysis

Real-time reactive price tracking and analysis across global retailers

Role

Project Manager (Team Lead)

Users

Global category managers

Ownership

End-to-end ownership from data crawl to front-end development, QC, and client-facing POC management

Business Impact

Annual revenue of $590K

The price tracking project monitors more than 5K SKUs across four regions — EMEA, NA, AU, and JP — covering 77 retailers. It tracks direct seller versus third-party prices, PDP prices, and in-cart prices.

Tracking runs twice a day, every day, enabling continuous views on price and stock status along with analysis, alerts, and actionable insights from daily movement across retailers and regions.

Executive Summary

High-level view of price, stock status, and core retailer tracking KPIs.

Price Analytics View

Drilldown into retailer pricing patterns, comparisons, and daily movement analysis.

Availability & SKU Drilldown

SKU-level view of availability and stock tracking across retailers and regions.
